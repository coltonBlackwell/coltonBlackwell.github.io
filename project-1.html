Koi Kapers

Koi Kapers is a 2D Java-implemented game that was inspired by the SFU koi pond with the pylons and chairs being tossed into it last winter. In this game, the user plays as the white koi fish attempting to escape the maze while avoiding contact with the moving enemy (swimming raccoon). Initial Design of Koi Kapers Vs.
